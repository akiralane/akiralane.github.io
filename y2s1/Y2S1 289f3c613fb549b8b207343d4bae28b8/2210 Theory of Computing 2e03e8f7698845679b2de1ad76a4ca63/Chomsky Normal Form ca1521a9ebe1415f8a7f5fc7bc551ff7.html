A CFG is in Chomsky Normal Form when all productions are of the form

A→BCA\rarr BCA→BC﻿ or A→aA \rarr aA→a﻿

That is, all non-terminals either produce precisely two non-terminals or a single terminal.

A CFG is in Greibach Normal Form when all productions are of the form

A→aB1B2…BkA \rarr aB_1 B_2 \dots B_kA→aB1​B2​…Bk​﻿ for k≥0k\geq0k≥0﻿

That is, all non-terminals produce a terminal followed by zero or more non-terminals.

Normal Form Theorem

For any CFG GGG﻿, there exists a CFG G′G'G′﻿ in Chomsky Normal Form such that L(G′)=L(G)−{ε}L(G')=L(G)-\{\varepsilon\}L(G′)=L(G)−{ε}﻿

That is, we can convert any CFG into one which is in Chomsky Normal Form excluding the empty string. The exclusion is there because CNF can't produce the empty string.

ε\varepsilonε﻿ and unit productions

A production A→εA\rarr \varepsilonA→ε﻿ is called an ε\varepsilonε﻿-production.

A production A→BA\rarr BA→B﻿ (from a non-terminal to another single non-terminal) is called a unit-production.

The idea is that, for some GGG﻿, there exists a CFG G′G'G′﻿ with no ε\varepsilonε﻿ or unit rules such that

L(G′)=L(G)−{ε}L(G')=L(G)-\{\varepsilon\}L(G′)=L(G)−{ε}﻿

The actual proof of this lemma is a hell-proof which I don't think you need to understand.

Algorithm for converting to CNF

  1. Remove all ε\varepsilonε﻿ and unit productions.
    1. The ε\varepsilonε﻿ productions can just be removed; the unit productions can be "squished together" to remove the redundant transitive rules.
  1. For each terminal a∈Σa\in\Sigmaa∈Σ﻿, add a new non-terminal NaN_aNa​﻿ to the set of non-terminals NNN﻿.
  1. Replace every terminal aaa﻿ in any production PPP﻿ with NaN_aNa​﻿.
  1. Add productions Na→aN_a \rarr aNa​→a﻿ to PPP﻿.
  1. While there exists a production which results in more than two non-terminals (A→B1B2…BkA\rarr B_1B_2\dots B_kA→B1​B2​…Bk​﻿ with k>2k\gt2k>2﻿)
    1. Remove that production from PPP﻿.
    1. Add a new non-terminal CCC﻿ to the set of non-terminals NNN﻿.
    1. Add productions A→B1CA\rarr B_1CA→B1​C﻿ and C→B2…BkC\rarr B_2\dots B_kC→B2​…Bk​﻿ to the set of productions PPP﻿.
