The process

As you know, we deliver increments of the system through development sprints.

  1. Envision
    1. Produce product backlog
  1. Development iteration
    1. Plan sprint
      1. Produce and prioritise sprint backlog
      1. Prepare sprint
    1. Sprint
      1. Check progress, possibly through scrums
      1. Show progress - burndown chart
      1. Deliver increment
    1. Review sprint
  1. Repeat Development iteration until done.

Step 1: Envision

Write down everything you can think of to create a backlog of work - this is almost always more than enough for a first sprint.

The backlog then grows and changes as you learn more about the product and its customers.

Step 2b: Sprint

How do we know how we‚Äôre doing? Use scrums.

At least two per week, but preferably every day. 10-15 minutes, go through each team member.

Step 2c: Review

How did we do?

This naturally leads into reprioritisation and the planning for the next sprint.
